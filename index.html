New Coronavirus (covid-19) cases - South East region

This page summarises the number of diagnosed coronavirus (covid-19) cases recorded daily by Public Health England (PHE).

The PHE dashboard presents the number of cases, recovered patients, and deaths nationally as well as daily cumulative counts of cases for NHS Regions and Upper Tier Local Authorities and Unitary Authorities.

Data collection started on the 9th March 2020 and is usually updated at 6pm each day for the previous days figures.

This page works best in certain browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, and Safari, and does not work so well currently in Internet Explorer.

In some cases, number of diagnosed cases can go down.

As at the beginning of April, there is vast differences amoung the regions local authorities with Hampshire and Surrey having more than 600 cases in total whilst the Isle of Wight has less than 20 (as of 2nd April). The colour key below has been chosen to try and capture the breadth of new cases across the region, whilst being sensitive to small numbers as well as periods of no reported cases.

The colour key may change as the number of cases are expected to increase in coming weeks.
