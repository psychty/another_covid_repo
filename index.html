Coronavirus (Covid-19) local summary - Sussex and the South East region

Temporary notice

As the COVID-19 pandemic has progressed, the volume of new information has increased exponentially with frequent changes to the data formats, as well as new caveats for understanding the data. Processing this information is becomming a task too difficult to maintain and this has largely been done in my own time.

I have had to prioritise work on a local version of this analysis (https://wsx-c19-weekly-supplement.netlify.app) which is updated every day as new data is released. The West Sussex version does contain some comparisons with other areas (including maps) but it is focused primarily on the districts and boroughs in the county only.

For the time being, I will not be updating this version, which showed West Sussex and other Local Authorities in the South East of England. There may be an opportunity in the future for me to catch up with the changes and present a view comparing areas. Many Local Authorities are now publishing their own weekly updates of the local pictures and these provide valuable interpretations of the data so I would recommend searching for your local authority webpages and sign up to alerts.

Please do contact me via my github page below if you rely on these figures and I can try and help.
